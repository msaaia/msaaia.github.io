About Us

Msaaia Enterprises, based in Wazirabad, Pakistan, specializes in high-quality stainless steel flatware. Wazirabad, known for centuries of craftsmanship, is the heart of Pakistan’s cutlery industry. We offer premium flatware crafted from 304 stainless steel in elegant silver and gold finishes, ensuring durability, elegance, and sophistication for every dining occasion.

Terms & Policies

To maintain the highest quality standards and ensure a seamless wholesale experience, we follow these terms and conditions:

  • Minimum order 100 pieces, maximum order 500 per item.
  • Brand name engraving available for $110.
  • Orders can be placed via email or WhatsApp (details on the website).
  • Production takes 2 weeks from order confirmation.
  • Shipping time varies between 4 to 8 weeks depending on the destination.
  • 80% advance payment required at order confirmation, with the remaining 20% before shipment.
  • Delivery charges are the buyer’s responsibility, and the shipping partner is selected based on location.
  • Cancellations are not accepted once production begins.
  • Returns are only accepted for manufacturing defects reported within 3 days of delivery.
